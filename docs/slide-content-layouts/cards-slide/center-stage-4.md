# Center Stage (4 Items)

## Overview
A centred layout displaying four content cards in a row in the middle of the slide. Like Center Stage but for a quartet of items that share the spotlight equally. The surrounding whitespace frames the four cards as a cohesive group, ideal for presenting four key stats, features, or a quick process overview. Can be used with or without a slide header.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐

│  └────────┴────────┴────────┴─────┘ │
│                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## When to Use
- Presenting four key metrics or KPIs
- Four features, capabilities, or highlights
- A four-step process or workflow overview
- Four product options or comparison items
- Quarterly or seasonal summaries (Q1-Q4)
- Any slide where exactly four items should share centre-stage focus

## Content Recommendations
- **Card 1, Card 2, Card 3 & Card 4** (each):
  - Metric card with value, label, and optional delta
  - Feature card with icon, title, and short description
  - Process step with number and brief detail
  - Product or plan summary
  - Quarterly data point or seasonal metric
  - Compact comparison item
